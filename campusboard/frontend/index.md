## What is CampusBoard?

CampusBoard is a free digital bulletin board for university students in Turkey. Students post and discover listings in organized categories — lecture notes, internships, housing, second-hand items, campus events, and general announcements. Access requires a verified university email address (.edu.tr), ensuring a trusted, student-only environment.

Currently serving Istanbul Arel University. Expansion to additional Turkish universities is in progress.

---

## Categories

- **Ders Notu (Lecture Notes):** Share PDFs, summaries, past exam questions, and study materials with classmates.
- **Staj (Internship):** Post or find internship and part-time job opportunities suited to specific faculties.
- **Yurt / Kiralik Oda (Housing):** Find or list dormitory rooms, apartments, and rentals near campus.
- **İkinci El (Second-Hand):** Buy and sell books, calculators, tablets, laptops, and other student items safely within the campus community.
- **Kayıp / Bulundu (Lost & Found):** Report or find lost items such as student cards, headphones, and water bottles.
- **Etkinlik (Events):** Announce and discover club meetups, seminars, workshops, and campus events.
- **Genel (General):** Study groups, volunteer calls, department news, and anything that doesn't fit the above.

---

## Key Features

- Post a listing in under 2 minutes — select a category, add a title and description, optionally add an image
- Filter by category and faculty to quickly narrow down relevant listings
- Ask questions or leave comments directly under any listing; the listing owner is notified and can reply
- Save listings to Favorites for later reference
- Manage your own listings: publish, update, or close them
- Mobile-friendly — works in any browser, no app download required

---

## How It Works

1. Create an account with your university email address (.edu.tr)
2. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox
3. Post a listing by selecting a category, entering a title and description, and optionally uploading an image
4. Other students can find your listing by browsing or filtering, ask questions in comments, and contact you

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is CampusBoard free?**
Yes, completely free. No ads, no subscriptions.

**Who can use CampusBoard?**
Any student with a valid university email address (.edu.tr). Currently supporting Istanbul Arel University; more universities being added.

**How do I post a listing?**
Register at campusboard.app/register, log in, and click "İlan Ver" (Post a Listing). Choose a category, write a title and description, and publish.

**Can I ask questions about a listing?**
Yes. Open any listing and use the comments/Q&A section at the bottom to ask a question. The listing owner will be notified and can reply.

**How do I save a listing?**
Click the heart/bookmark icon on any listing card or detail page to add it to your Favorites.

**Is there a mobile app?**
No app download needed. CampusBoard is fully responsive and works in your mobile browser at campusboard.app.

**What happens when I find what I need?**
You can close your own listing once the need is fulfilled, so it no longer appears in search results.
